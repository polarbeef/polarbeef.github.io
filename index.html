Love is in the air for February, the month of love. Valentine's day is the time where everyone can express love spending time with their families, friends or a special someone. Chocolates and flowers are the most famous things that people give to each other. Even though the 21st century is the age of advanced technology, another thing that people give during Valentine's day are love letters that are written by hand.


Love letters are a way to express love to someone. Love letters contain feelings from the one who wrote it. Love letter can be in a form of paper, or an online message. Love letters can be in an expression of admiration, gratitude, longing, or courtship. Love letters can be for your family, friends, relatives, or for a special someone.
